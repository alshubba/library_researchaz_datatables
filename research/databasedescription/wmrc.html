GlobalInsight (Formerly World Markets Research Centre (WMRC)) is the leading global provider of business-critical country and industry intelligence and is relied on by thousands of executives in hundreds of multinational corporations, financial institutions and governments across the world. GlobalInsight assesses and forecasts political, economic, regulatory/legal, tax, operational, security and general business conditions in 196 countries. Acquired by Global Insight, Inc (recognized globally as the #1 commercial economic forecasting firm) in April 2003, GlobalInsight now delivers the most comprehensive and systematically up-to-date country analysis and data available. Our insightful analysis of market conditions and key events around the world helps companies to assess risk, make informed decisions and seize business opportunities in both their domestic and foreign markets.
