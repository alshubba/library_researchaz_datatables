Project MUSE is an online resource providing nearly 250 scholarly journals from 40 publishers. Members of the Bentley community now have access to some of the most important academic resources in the arts, humanities and social sciences. Journals can be searched by title, subject, or collection.

MUSE offers articles in full text (HTML and PDF formats), in a variety of subject areas including, economics, education, film, history, literature, philosophy, politics, religion, women's studies and more.

Users can sign up for Journal Alerts to be notified when new issues of their favorite journals are added to the database. Muse will e-mail users the table of contents of their selected journals.

Search results can be managed in several ways, including e-mail citations that link to full-text articles, printer friendly formatted articles and support for citation management software such as EndNote© and RefWorks©.

Search features include the option of searching the entire database or within specific subject ranges. Users can also search the full text of articles or by subject, author, or title.
