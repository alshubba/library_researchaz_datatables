The Bentley Library offers access to Academic Search Premier, Business Searching Interface, Business Source Premier, Communication and Mass Media Complete, ERIC, and MEDLINEfrom EBSCOHost. These databases allow users to search the most recent periodicals, with an emphasis on refereed and scholarly publications from a multitude of disciplines.

Academic Search Premier
One of the largest academic multi-disciplinary database, Academic Search Premier provides full text for nearly 4,000 scholarly publications, including full text for nearly 3,100 peer-reviewed journals. Coverage spans virtually every area of academic study and offers information dating as far back as 1975. This database is updated on a daily basis via EBSCO host.

Business Searching Interface
The Business Searching Interface allows users to search for Business Source Premier content via a dynamic interconnection designed to highlight business and industry resources.Â  BSI users can take advantage over 30 limiters to help narrow the focus of complex research questions related to companies, industries, products, services, and general business from peer reviewed journals to books to newspapers.

Business Source Premier
Business Source Premier provides full text for nearly 3,300 scholarly business journals, including full text for more than 1000 peer-reviewed business publications. Coverage includes virtually all subject areas related to business. This database provides full text (PDF) for more than 300 of the top scholarly journals dating as far back as 1922. This database is updated on a daily basis.

Communication and Mass Media Complete
Communication & Mass Media Complete provides research in areas related to communication and mass media. CMMC incorporates CommSearch (formerly produced by the National Communication Association) and Mass Media Articles Index (formerly produced by Penn State University) along with numerous other journals to create a research and reference resource of wide scope and depth in the communication and mass media fields.

ERIC
ERIC, the Educational Resource Information Center contains more than 2,200 digests along with references for additional information and citations and abstracts from over 980 educational and education-related journals.

MEDLINE
MEDLINE provides authoritative medical information on medicine, nursing, dentistry, veterinary medicine, the health care system, pre-clinical sciences, and much more. Created by the National Library of Medicine, MEDLINE allows users to search abstracts from over 4,600 current biomedical journals. Includes data through 9/03/03.
