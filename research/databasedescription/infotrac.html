SearchBank is the Web version of Infotrac. Bentley's subscription to SearchBank includes access to Expanded Academic ASAP, Business and Company Resource Center, General Business File ASAP, Contemporary Authors, Contemporary Literary Criticism, General Reference Center (Magazine Index), Health Reference Center, and Predicast PROMT.

Expanded Academic ASAP (1980-present), indexes more than 1,500 periodicals, with more than 500 being full-text.  Subjects covered include astronomy, religion, law, history, psychology, humanities, current events, sociology, communications, and the general sciences.

General Business File ASAP (1996-present), indexes more than 900 periodicals, with more than 450 being full-text. Subjects covered include company profiles, finance, international trade, money management, business trends, investments, and banking.

Business and Company Resource Center 1980 - Jan 2001 with access to back file (1980 - 1997). Use this database to find articles on: finance, acquisitions and mergers, international trade, money management, new technologies and products, local and regional business trends, investments and banking.

General Reference Center -- Magazine Index (1995-present, with a back-file from 1980-1994), indexes more than 415 general interest magazines, with more than 300 being full-text.  Coverage also includes a number of ready-reference almanacs and encyclopedia.

Predicast PROMT (1996-present), indexes nearly 1,000 business and trade journals, industry newsletters, market research studies, news releases and investment reports. Use this database to do research on companies, the products and technologies they produce, and the markets in which the compete.

Contemporary Authors provides biographic and bibliographic information for 100,000 international and U.S. authors.

Contemporary Literary Criticism -- Select provides an extensive collection of critical essays on contemporary authors. CLC -- Select entries contain biographical and bibliographic information for authors.

Dictionary of Literary Biography
Dictionary of Literary Biography provides essays on the lives of authors from all eras and genres. Essays in the DLB are written by major scholars and provide bibliographic information on each author's writings as well as sources for further reading.
