Mediamark Research Inc. is the leading provider of magazine audience data to the advertising and marketing communities. MRI+/Mediamark consolidates, in one convenient place, many of the databases used to evaluate magazines or seeking directory and editorial information. It has a powerful search and access capability, plus a cost planning module. 

New users of MRI+ will be asked to provide a Bentley University e-mail address (shortname@bentley.edu) and register for an account to access to the database.  Patrons who previously used the print or CD-ROM versions of the Mediamark Reporter can now get electronic access to this title on the MRI+/Mediamark site.
