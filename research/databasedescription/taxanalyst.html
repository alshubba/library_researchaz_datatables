Tax Analysts Federal Tax Research Libraries provides access to a wide range of tax information. Federal tax information include the Basic Federal Research Library, the Chief Council Advice Library and the Court Opinions Library. State tax information includes state related statues and laws, administrative rulings and state tax tables.

Searching the Tax Analyst Libraries:

  1. Click on the hypetext link to the Tax Analysts Federal Tax Research Libraries
  2. Then select the oppropriate section (Federal or State) on the upper left of the screen
  3. Ignore the 'Free Trial' and login information in the center of the login webpage.
