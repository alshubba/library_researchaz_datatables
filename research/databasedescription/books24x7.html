ITPro offers over 4200 IT-related e-Book titles, in full-text. 70% of the titles in ITPro were published after 2000.

BusinessPro offers over 2400 Business-related ebook titles, in full-text. BusinessPro adds an average of 50 titles a month to the collection. BusinessPro offers Case Studies, text-books and popular titles, on all aspects of business and the business process.

On-campus users: To access BusinessPro/ITPro from on-campus, users must first create an account with Books24x7.com from a Bentley University computer. Creating an account with Books24x7.com allows users to save searches and results in the "My Bookshelf" section. It also allows users to sign-up for e-mail notifications when new titles are added to the database.

Off-campus users: Members of the Bentley Community can access Books24x7.com remotely by using a Bentley University Internet Password. Please Note: Users who access Books24x7.com remotely are logged into the database as anonymous users and cannot access previous searches saved in the "My Bookshelf" section.

Users who do not know how to set an Internet Password in Banner Web or the Lotus Notes Address book please visit the Owl Knowledge Base.
